## Practice Area Quick Reference

### Startup & Corporate
**When to use:** Entity formation, shareholder agreements, fundraising, corporate tax  
**Key laws:** 
- Commercial Companies Law (Federal Decree-Law No. 32/2021, amended by No. 20/2025)
- Corporate Tax Law (Federal Decree-Law No. 47/2022)
- DIFC Companies Law No. 5/2018
- ADGM Companies Regulations 2020

**Details:** → `references/startup-corporate.md`

### Healthcare Compliance
**When to use:** Facility licensing, professional licensing, home healthcare, advertising  
**Key authorities:**
- DHA (Dubai) — Sheryan portal
- DOH (Abu Dhabi) — TAMM portal
- MOHAP (Northern Emirates) — Sharik portal

**Key laws:**
- Federal Law No. 4/2015 (Private Health Facilities, amended 2023)
- Federal Law No. 4/2016 (Medical Liability)
- PQR v3 (Unified professional qualifications)

**Details:** → `references/healthcare.md`

### Contract Review
**When to use:** Reviewing, drafting, or negotiating commercial contracts  
**Key principles:**
- Article 246 (Good faith) — Applies to ALL contracts, cannot contract out
- Article 257 (Freedom of contract) — Subject to law, public policy, Sharia

**Key law:** UAE Civil Code (Federal Law No. 5/1985)

**Details:** → `references/contracts.md`

### Employment Law
**When to use:** Hiring, termination, gratuity, disputes, Emiratisation  
**Key laws:**
- UAE Labour Law (Federal Decree-Law No. 33/2021)
- 2024 Amendments (Federal Decree-Law No. 9/2024)

**Critical updates (2024):**
- MOHRE binding decisions for claims ≤AED 50,000
- Salary continuation during disputes (up to 2 months)
- Limitation period extended to 2 years

**Details:** → `references/employment.md`

## Critical Rules (Always Apply)

### Good Faith (Article 246, UAE Civil Code)
Every UAE contract must be performed in good faith. Courts can:
- Imply terms based on good faith
- Modify or refuse to enforce clauses exercised in bad faith
- Award damages under Article 106 (abuse of rights)

**You cannot contract out of good faith.**

### Gratuity Formula (Article 51, Labour Law)
```
First 5 years: 21 days' basic salary × years
After 5 years: 30 days' basic salary × years
Maximum: 2 years' total basic salary
```
**Basic salary only** — excludes housing, transport, other allowances.

### Healthcare Jurisdiction
- Dubai operations → DHA license required
- Abu Dhabi operations → DOH license required (separate from DHA)
- Expansion requires **new licenses** in each jurisdiction
